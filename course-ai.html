Course Overview

This course introduces the fundamental concepts of artificial intelligence. You'll cover search algorithms, knowledge representation, reasoning under uncertainty, machine learning (supervised, unsupervised, reinforcement), and neural networks. Practical labs will give you hands-on experience with AI libraries and tools.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

  • Implement classic AI search algorithms (BFS, DFS, A*, minimax).
  • Represent knowledge using logical and probabilistic methods.
  • Apply supervised learning algorithms (linear regression, decision trees, SVM).
  • Understand unsupervised learning (clustering, PCA).
  • Build and train simple neural networks using TensorFlow/PyTorch.
  • Discuss ethical issues and future trends in AI.
